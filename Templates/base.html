THE INTERSURE EXPERIENCE

Intersure is a group of independent insurance brokers that have partnered to share ideas, help each other grow, and above all, challenge each other.
We are an association like no other. Our member companies are committed to each other at the deepest level and share openly — knowing that what’s said between partners is confidential.
We facilitate a rare opportunity to really “get under the hood” of how similar (but non-competing) firms are operating and network with like-minded professionals who can relate to the challenges you’re facing.
There is no fluff, smoke and mirrors, or grandstanding at Intersure –- and this is the core of what makes our organization great. The way we come together is what sets us apart.
In short, we’re:
All Together. Different.
